# 365-Coffee-Sales-Analytics
Project OverviewI developed this interactive Excel Dashboard to transform raw cafe transaction data into actionable business insights. This project focuses on identifying growth trends, operational efficiencies, and product performance to drive data-backed decision-making.  
🔍 Key Business InsightsSteady Growth: 
The business maintains a positive upward trend with a 15.31% CAGR-based monthly growth rate.  
Operational Peak: Identified 7:00 AM to 10:00 AM as the peak activity window, crucial for staffing and inventory management.
Core Drivers: Coffee, Tea, and Bakery contribute to ~85% of total transactions (126,661+ orders).  
Growth Opportunity: Discovered that the Branded category is underperforming in the Tipu Sultan area, holding only a 15.93% share.  
🛠️ Technical Skills :
DemonstratedData Cleaning: Structuring raw transactional data for analysis.
Advanced Excel: Utilization of Pivot Tables, KPI Metrics, and complex Formulas (CAGR calculations).  
Data Visualization: Designing a decision-ready dashboard with interactive charts.
Performance Analysis: Time-based (Hourly/Weekly) and Category-wise deep dives.
